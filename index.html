PROCESS

How we can collaborate in 5 easy steps.

STEP 1 | PROJECT REQUEST

At the beginning of our collaboration is the project request. Your information helps me to understand if I am the right person for your project. If yes, we will arrange a video call so I can learn more.

STEP 2 | FIRST MEETING

When working together, trust is essential. During our initial meeting, we get to know each other and figure out if we are a good fit; then I clarify any questions and define the next steps.

STEP 3 | PROPOSAL

After I have learned as much about your project as possible, I create an individual proposal that itemizes all of my services. This way you have a full overview of the costs associated with building your website or brand.

STEP 4 | IMPLEMENTATION

When I have all the materials that I need, I immediately begin implementation. Transparency and effective communication are key to my process. I'll keep you informed of the process through regular updates.

STEP 5 | LAUNCH / HANDOVER

Before it's launched, the finished website goes through an internal quality assurance process. Once it's finished, we publish!¬†
